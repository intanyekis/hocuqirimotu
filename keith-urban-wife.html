A Whirlwind Romance and a Fairytale Wedding

Urban and Kidman instantly fell in love and moved in together after their first date. They first appeared together in public at the 2006 Grammy Awards, where Urban won Best Male Country Vocal Performance.

Urban proposed to Kidman in May 2006 with a 3.5-carat diamond ring. They married on June 25, 2006, in front of 230 guests, including Hugh Jackman, Naomi Watts, and Russell Crowe, at the Cardinal Cerretti Memorial Chapel in Sydney, Australia.

Kidman wore a Balenciaga gown and her mother’s veil, while Urban sported a black suit and a rose corsage. The pair exchanged traditional vows and rings, as well as a duet of Rodney Crowell’s “Making Memories of Us,” which Urban recorded. They then celebrated with a reception at Kidman’s friend and business partner,Andrew Kidman’s, neighboring property.

The marriage of Urban and Kidman was not without difficulties. Urban enrolled in a treatment center for the third time in October 2006, barely four months after their wedding, to treat his alcohol problem. Later, he acknowledged using cocaine and ecstasy as well.
