Bobby Wood (Politician) Obituary, Funeral

Bobby G. Wood was an American politician. Wood was born on September 21, 1935 and died on January 12, 2023. He was 87 years.

Wood was born in Fyffe, Alabama.

He lived in Harrison, Tennessee, with his wife and family, and was involved with the insurance business and the Chattanooga Glass Company.

In politics, Wood served in the Tennessee House of Representatives from 1976 to 2004 and was a Republican.

State Senator Bo Watson paid tribute to Wood, singling him out for his ability to work with Democrats.
