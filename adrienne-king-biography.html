Adrienne King Bio (Wiki)

Who is Adrienne King?

Adrienne King is an American film, stage, and voice actress, former stuntwoman, and visual artist. She is widely recognized for her role as Alice Hardy in Sean S. Cunningham’s horror film ‘Friday the 13th’ in 1980.

Adrienne King: Age, Parents, Siblings, Nationality, Ethnicity

Adrienne King was born on July 21, 1955, in Oyster Bay, New York, USA. She is currently 65 years old. Her mother was English and from Liverpool and Adrienne is a first-generation American.

At the age of six months old, she appeared in her first commercial. Regarding siblings, she has a brother named John and a sister named Laura. She is the surrogate daughter of Betsy Palmer.

Adrienne holds American citizenship but her ethnicity is unknown.

Education, School/College University

Talking about her education history, she attended the London Royal Academy. Also, she studied art at New York’s Fashion Institute of Technology.

Adrienne King: Professional Life and Career

Adrienne King made her appearance in the television film ‘Inherit the Wind’ in 1965 at the age of five without a supporting role. She began auditioning in New York City when she was in ninth grade. She performed as an uncredited dancer in the films Saturday Night Fever’ in 1977 and ‘Hair’ in 1979.

After two years, she was referred to producer Sean S. Cunningham through a mutual friend. Then, she appeared in his film ‘Friday the 13th’. By the following year, she appeared as Alice Hardy in the sequel ‘Friday the 13th Part 2’ and starred in a Downy commercial. Later, she was hired as a stunt performer and background actor for the film ‘Ghostbusters’ in 1984.

Adrienne King did voice acting and ADR work for ‘The Man Without Face’, Philadelphia’, ‘Wolf’, ‘And Titanic’. She made her first on-screen film appearance in the science fiction/horror film ‘Psychic Experiment’  in 27 years.

Recently, she appeared as Theresa in ‘Admonition’ in 2018, Jackie Winters in ‘William Frost in 2018, and Mrs. Perkins in ‘Killer Therapy’ in 2019.
