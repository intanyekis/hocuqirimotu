Elvis Presley's Granddaughter Riley Keough Marries Ben Smith-Petersen

What a week for surprise weddings!

Riley Keough and Ben Smith-Petersen tied the knot on Wednesday night in an intimate ceremony in Napa, California, according to multiple reports. The newlyweds exchanged vows in front of celebrity friends including Kristen Stewart, Cara Delevingne, ZoÃ« Kravitz, Abby Lee, and Courtney Eaton according to E! Online. Keough, the 25-year-old granddaughter of Elvis Presley, met the Australian stuntman on the set of Mad Max: Fury Road. They were first spotted out together in January 2014 and announced their engagement in August.

"So that happened," Smith-Petersen captioned an image of the couple is lying in bed, with Riley flashing her new bling.

A few months later Keough celebrated her bachelorette party at the Madonna Inn in San Luis Obisbo, California. Her BFF Kristen Stewart was also in attendance.

Before Smith-Petersen, Keough was consistently (and falsely) rumored to be dating Robert Pattinson in 2013.

"I don't want to say who she's with or not; I will say who she's not with and that is a complete lie, and the silliest thing I've ever heard, is that she was with Robert Pattinson because her and Kristen Stewart are like best friends," Lisa Marie Presley told Andy Cohen on Watch What Happens Live. "So that was the craziest rumor that I will put to bed."
