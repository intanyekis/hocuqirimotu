Ben Affleck Spotted Without His Wedding Ring And Fuels Rumors That Theyre Headed For Divorce

Highlights

  • Ben Affleck spotted without a wedding ring as rumors of marriage trouble with Jennifer Lopez continue to swirl.
  • Conflicting reports regarding their living situation, but the couple was seen together at their children's school event.
  • Despite issues, Affleck and Lopez continue to present a united front for their kids, publicly supporting them.

Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopez have yet to make a statement confirming or denying the relationship rumors about them, but some fans will feel that the evidence that there is trouble in paradise is stacking up. This week alone, there has been a report that the actor has left their shared marital home and is searching for a new place. Now, photographers have also seen that Affleck is no longer wearing his wedding ring.

Affleck was recently photographed in his car (the pictures were obtained by TMZ) when he made a trip to his children's school. The pictures focused on his hand, and he appeared to have no wedding ring.

Ben Affleck Is No Longer Wearing His Wedding Ring

Ben Affleck's lack of a wedding ring is a big deal because, while there have been reports about their marriage problems for some time now, TMZ notes that taking off his wedding ring was a recent development. As for Jennifer Lopez, the outlet was unable to comment on whether she was still wearing her ring as the last time she was seen in public was May 16, 2024 (and there were no close-up photos of her ring finger).

There have been conflicting reports about what is going on with this couple and why they could potentially be having problems. A source told Us Weekly earlier this week that they have not ended their marriage. "Jen and Ben are having issues in their marriage," the insider said. "They started having issues a few months ago as Jen had started ramping up work commitments and prepping for her tour." Another source also alleged that they were working on their marriage. This is why the news that they could potentially not be wearing their wedding rings is such a big deal!

What Has Been Said About Jennifer Lopez And Ben Affleck's Marriage?

It is believed that Ben Affleck recently left the home he shares with Jennifer Lopez. However, a new report from Us Weekly appears to conflict with this timeline. A source told the publication that the Argo actor had left the home "several weeks ago." It had previously been reported that Affleck is living at home in Brentwood, California (and he was photographed by TMZ coming and going). Despite this, the couple was seen attending a school play for Fin Affleck. Lopez's child, Emme Muniz, was also in attendance. Of their meetup, a source told InTouch Weekly that they had come together for their kids.

"Jen looked super thin and was dressed casual-chic. She didn't glam up like the last time she came to a school function," an eyewitness said. "She seemed more subdued than usual when she greeted Ben as he pulled the car up. Emme was super excited, though, jumping for joy over Fin's performance. Nothing would have stopped them coming together for the kids."
