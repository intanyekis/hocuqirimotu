This Might Be Drake's Best Interview Ever

Drizzy Drake and Noah “40” Shebib knew what to expect before their latest interview with Nardwuar, but they found out real quick that the Human Serviette went deeper than they could ever imagine.

READ: Drake’s ‘VIEWS’ Hits 1 Million In Sales

From the very house that Drake spent summers in with his father in Memphis to the first time the 6 God and superproducer met, there was no stone unturned in the hour long interview. Nardwuar was also nonstop with the gifts of old vinyl, cassettes and DVDs from some of the most influential artists in both their lives.

The interview features rare memorabilia from Biggie, Kool G Rap, Common, Timmy Thomas, Vybz Kartel and more. Watch on as two of hip-hop’s biggest names gaze on in amazement as Nardwuar stuns once again.
