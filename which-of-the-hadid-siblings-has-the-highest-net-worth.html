Which Of The Hadid Siblings Has The Highest Net Worth?

When it comes to the world of fashion and modeling, the Hadid name is widely recognized! Whether you are a fan of Gigi, Bella, or Anwar Hadid, there is no denying that this family belongs on the runway. The three siblings first came to prominence back in 2012 when their mother, and former supermodel, Yolanda Hadid joined the cast of 'The Real Housewives'. While they didn't have the most prominent role in the show, they were often shot together as a family, showcasing just how close a unit they all were.

It didn't take much time before all three followed in their mom's footsteps, all becoming international models. Anwar, Bella, and the eldest, Gigi, who recently gave birth to her and Zayn Malik's first child, have modeled for some of the biggest names in fashion from Marc Jacobs, Tommy Hilfiger, Gucci, all the way to Jeremy Scott. With successful and thriving careers, here's which of the three Hadid siblings has the highest net worth.

Which Hadid Is Worth The Most?

After fans were first introduced to the Hadid family back on 'The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills', in 2012, it was clear that these three were destined for the runway. Much like their mother, Yolanda Hadid, who reigned as a supermodel throughout the '80s, Gigi and Bella Hadid, and now younger brother Anwar, have become recognized worldwide and have worked as models, brand ambassadors, and the face of countless campaigns for massive names like Tommy Hilfiger, Christian Dior, and Chanel, to name a few.

While modeling has easily been their claim to fame, the trio has gone on to do a number of exciting things from romance, pregnancy, and business ventures outside of their ties to the fashion and beauty world. Anwar Hadid, the youngest of the bunch, is currently dating singer, Dua Lipa. The two first started dating back in 2013 but broke things off. Luckily, they rekindled their flame again in 2018 and have been inseparable since.

As for Bella, she too has been in a number of high profile relationships, including her time with The Weeknd. Both Anwar and Bella have had successful careers, with Anwar coming in with a net worth of $4 million and Bella with a staggering net worth of $25 million, however, it is Gigi who comes out on top!

Gigi Hadid comes in at a net worth of $29 million, making her the highest-earning sibling of the three! The model had an earlier start to modeling than Bella and Anwar did, giving her a bit of an advantage when it comes to their collective $58 million net worth. Additionally, Gigi secured her first modeling gig as a little kid, where she modeled for Baby Guess, and another time where she appeared in Vogue.

In 2012, Gigi was named the face of a Guess campaign, she later signed with IMG models and became Victoria's Secret angel all in the span of a couple of years! It was at this time that Bella and Anwar began their journey into modeling as well, proving that the Hadid gene pool is not to be messed with.
