Twitter Wants James Corden Removed From The Friends Reunion

The long-awaited Friends reunion is finally on the horizon and fans across the globe are truly struggling to contain their excitement as they wait out these last few days. The teaser for the big show has just been released, giving fans all the feels, and the 'feeling' that is most prominent right now is annoyance. Fans are furious that James Corden is part of this epic reunion, and of all the people they could have brought back for this milestone moment, Corden isn't the one fans want to see.

Twitter has ignited with fiery complaints about Corden being involved in this epic reunion, and fans want him removed as fast as possible. He's souring the overall experience and has the potential to have a very damaging effect on this important moment.

Twitter Has No Love For Corden

James Corden has nothing to do with the show and is not related to the legendary sitcom in any way. He's not being embraced at all. In fact, die-hard fans that are seeking their nostalgic fix are highly frustrated that he's tainting their experience.

Twitter is blowing up with rage, demanding that Corden be removed, and a more suitable person be put in his place.

Fans Aren't Into Corden

Another fan wrote in to say; "Can I have the ‘No James Corden Cut’ please? #FriendsReunion," while someone else said "Right? It really ticked me off hearing his voice at the end like if he were in any way affiliated with James Corden"

Someone cleverly wove the lyrics of the theme song in to reflect their feelings. They wrote; "So no one told me it was gonna be this way. James Cordon's hosting and that's all I've got to say. I won't be watching this show anymore."

There are so many other deserving people that appeared on the show and truly deserved to have a place in the reunion show. Opting for Corden seems to be a highly unnatural choice. Nostalgic, hopeful fans chimed in to remind the producers that Brad Pitt, Bruce Willis, Christina Applegate, and so many others stars actually made appearances on the set of Friends and would have been much better choices than Corden.

Twitter comments continued with; "I'm sorry if this isn't brand new information but WHY IN THE FRESH HELL IS JAMES CORDEN HOSTING PART OF THE FRIENDS REUNION???" Also: "once again i am asking... why out of all the hosts in the world.. is James Corden hosting the friends reunion? you literally could’ve had Oprah."

Oh, Hey Now...

Then, one fan seemed to nail it by solving the mystery. Ben Winston, the producer of the reunion show happens to be Corden's good pal.
