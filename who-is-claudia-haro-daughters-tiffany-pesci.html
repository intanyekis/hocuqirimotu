Who is Claudia Haro daughters Tiffany Pesci?

Claudia Martha Haro is an American former actress.

She is known for New Nightmare (1994), With Honors (1994) and Casino (1995).

Claudia was previously married to actor Joe Pesci and Film Director Garrett Warren.

On May 20, 2000, Haro’s ex-husband Garrett Warren was shot four times in the face and upper body by a gunman in his Westlake Village apartment in California. He survived but lost his right eye.

Claudia Haro was arrested on charges related to the crime in December 2005. She eventually pleaded no contest to two counts of attempted murder and a principal firearm charge for the attempted murder of Warren and in April 2012 was sentenced to 12 years and 4 months in prison.

She served her sentence at the California Institution for Women in Chino, California, and was released in August 2019.

Who is Claudia Haro daughter’s Tiffany Pesci?

Tiffany Pesci is an American model and socialite.

She is the daughter of former actress Claudia Haro and ex-husband Joe Pesci, an American actor and musician.

Tiffany Pesci born in 1992, stands at a height of 4 feet 8 inches (1.42m) tall.

Tiffany Pesci’s parents got married on September 7, 1988, but divorced in 1992 due to undisclosed reasons.
