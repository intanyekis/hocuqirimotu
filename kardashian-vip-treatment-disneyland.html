The Kardashians Pay For VIP Treatment At Disneyland

Kim and Khloe Kardashian spared no expense when treating their kids to a day at Disneyland earlier this week.

According to TMZ, the family spent at least $3,000 for VIP access to the iconic theme park. The outing was in honor of Khloe’s daughter True’s 4th birthday. The toddler was joined by her cousins Chicago, the youngest daughter of Kim Kardashian, as well as Dream, Rob Kardashian’s daughter.

As per Disneyland’s website, up to 9 guests can get a 7-hour VIP Tour starting at $2,975 USD. The luxurious package gives guests priority access, likely implying they can skip the notoriously long lines.

The VIP package also comes with a personal guide who promises to pay attention to your group’s needs while providing a tour complete with insider tips and stories.

Photos of the Kardashians show the family hopping on a variety of rides during their afternoon out. However, the outing wasn’t without controversy.

Since photos were published, Kim and Khloe have come under fire for skipping lines and hogging rides while regular guests were forced to wait their turn.

Mother and fellow Disney guest Rachael Kevin uploaded a video to TikTok detailing her experience seeing the Kardashians at the theme park. In the video, she shows how Kim, Khloe, and their group were allowed on the tea cup rides by themselves – leaving many of the cups empty.

“When the Kardashians cut the line, get the ride to themselves, and make us common folk wait and watch them… typical,” text on the video read.

“It seemed like forever,” Rachael continued. “We finally got on 30 minutes later.”

As Buzzfeed points out, not every celebrity demands the VIP treatment – or at least, fully empty rides – when visiting Disneyland. Recently, footage of Cardi B enjoying the teacup ride with her entourage went viral. The rapper can be seen riding in the cups alongside other park guests.

The Kardashian’s Disneyland outing wasn’t the only big thing they did to celebrate True’s birthday. The toddler also had a cat-themed party attended by her famous cousins. Photos from the event show the kids in face paint as well as an adopt-a-(stuffed)-cat station, likely party favors for the kids.

Only a few months ago, the Kardashians threw an equally epic joint birthday party for Chicago and Stormi, the eldest child of Kylie Jenner, both of whom turned 4-years old. The girls had a Barbie and LOL Dolls-themed bash filled with elaborate pink décor, a bouncy castle, and a ball pit.
