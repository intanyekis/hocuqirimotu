Vanessa Hudgens' &quot;Just Here for the Boos&quot; Tee: Shop Her Style

It’s time! Living her best October life, Vanessa Hudgens stepped out to Knott’s Scary Farm’s “black carpet” event on Thursday, Oct. 1, in Buena Park, Calif., wearing an adorable Halloween-ready ensemble with a very direct message.

The High School Musical alum, 26, rocked a yellow cardigan, metallic short shorts, ankle booties, and one fabulous tee reading, “Just Here for the Boos.”

Hudgens, who recently wrapped a stint in Gigi on Broadway, also added a little costume-y touch to her outfit: cat ears, which she appropriately paired with bold “cat eye” eyeliner (plus peach cheeks and a bronze lip).

On the carpet, the brunette beauty, who will be starring in the upcoming Grease: Live TV special as Rizzo, posed with the event’s zombies, making sure to ham it up (if only for Instagram!). She later posted one of the snaps, playfully captioning it, “Where is this child’s parents? And why is she so angry? Maybe she’s hungry. HA.”
