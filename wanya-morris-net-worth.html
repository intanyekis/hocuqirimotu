Wanya Jermaine Morris is an American vocalist with an estimated net worth of $60 million. Wanya Morris started out as part of the group Unique Attraction, later turning it into a male based group Boys II Men. Morris. Wanya Morris along with singers Shawn Stockman and Nathan Morris were the members of the band.

The band gained great popularity in the 1990s and gained international success after the group's first single reached number one with their song "End of the Road" in 1992. "End of the Road" topped the worldwide charts while breaking the record for staying at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 for thirteen weeks in a row, breaking a record once held by Elvis Presley.

The band has had collaborations with various A-list celebrities such as Mariah Carey, and Morris' ex-girlfriend singer Brandy.

Along with being a part of the band Boys II Men, Morris has his own record label  "The Company Entertainment."
