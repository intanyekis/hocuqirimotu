Here Are 15 Ways He Spends His Money

Comedian, screenwriter, singer, writer, film producer...is there anything that Adam Sandler can’t do? The American actor, who is known for his impeccable comic timing and charming female co-stars, including Drew Barrymore and Jennifer Aniston, has made movies that are an important part of pop culture. Many of his films have resonated with audiences, including 50 First Dates, The Wedding Singer, Men, Women & Children, and Punch-Drunk Love.

While he's had his flops, his most successful movies really catapulted him to fame...they made him a household name. He's starred in groundbreaking films and he's worn multiple hats. His talents have made it possible for him to amass a shockingly huge fortune.

As of 2020, his net worth stands at a whopping $420 million - here are some of the interesting ways that he spends his money:

15 A Posh House

Adam Sandler has a luxurious villa with a swimming pool in California. The home was earlier owned by Goldie Hawn and Kurt Russell - it was sold to Adam for the massive sum of $12 million back in 2004. The property is a sprawling and gorgeous one, featuring almost 13,000 square feet.

14 More Properties Across The World

Adam Sandler’s home in California reportedly has seven bathrooms and double the number of bedrooms! The backyard is magnificent. Adam Sandler is known to have homes in various parts of the world, and some of them are located in Malibu, Boca Raton, and New Hampshire.

13 Vacations With Family

Every celebrity loves to take time off from work because vacationing (and spending quality time with family) is such a great way to reset and refresh. Adam Sandler is no exception. Recently, he was spotted vacationing with his wife and children in Miami. He seemed to be having a blast with his daughters on some hair-raising rides. In the past, pictures of Adam on an Australian beach with his daughters also went viral.

12 Homes That Spell Splendor

The comedian likes to live life king-size - this is evident from his homes, all of which have unique features. The Malibu property he had built for him really showcases his extravagant lifestyle. The home boasts a patio at beach level, plus plenty of other upscale amenities. Naturally, the home is on an exclusive street.

11 Shoe Fetish

Adam Sandler isn't exactly a fashionista when it comes to his wardrobe. He is often spotted out and about in the city, looking like the boy next door, thanks to his graphic tees and shorts. But he does seem to have a thing for fancy shoes. In February of 2020, he stepped out to watch a Lakers game wearing high-end shoes! They were said to be priced at $400! That's a lot, but some celebs spend more!

10 Cozy And Luxe

Other than the properties mentioned above, Adam Sandler also owns a home in Miami. The Florida house, which resembles a palace, has an old-world charm to it. Adam must love its well-manicured gardens that look like a golf course. Don't you love the palm trees waving in the breeze?

9 A Generous Friend

If there is truly a generous friend in Hollywood, it is Adam Sandler. Such is the generosity of the actor that he has spent lavishly on his co-stars to make them feel valued and motivated. Adam has given luxurious cars to some of his co-stars, including David Spade, to express admiration for their work.

8 A Collection of Fancy Cars

Adam Sandler likes to drive in style, and his collection of cars speaks volumes about his love for all things luxury. He owns a Dodger Challenger and is also the proud owner of a Hybrid SUV. One of the highest earners in Hollywood, he gave Maseratis to his co-stars from Grown Ups.

7 Social Causes

While he likes to spend a fortune on the good things in life, Adam Sandler ensures he spends his money on good causes as well. A few years ago, he donated $1 million to the Boys and Girls Club in his hometown. Later, he donated $2,100 to a presidential campaign.

6 A Soft Spot For Children

Adam Sandler is known to contribute generously to causes that help children, such as charities that assist kids with autism. Toys for Tots is another charity that Adam helps out. Adam also deserves praise for donating 400 PlayStations to the children of war-torn Israel.

5 A Crazy Basketball Fan

Adam Sandler takes a keen interest in the sport of basketball. He is an expert worth watching when it comes to dribbling the ball. He is also a regular spectator at basketball matches and tournaments. The paparazzi have clicked him on such visits multiple times.

4  Splurging On Expensive Holidays

Hawaii is among the most fun (but also the most pricey) holiday destinations in the USA and the world. Adam Sandler, who is an out and out family man, does not mind spending big bucks on a big, fat vacation. He is often spotted with his family, having a great time on the beaches of Maui.

3 The Party Man

Adam Sandler loves to party big. In 2013, he threw a big bash for the who’s who of Hollywood. The party was so grand that it made headlines and was one of the most talked-about events for quite some time. Hosted at Pickwick Gardens in California, the party featured some famous guests, including Bella Thorne, Marilyn Manson, and Demi Moore!

2 Grand Affair

The event was organized by Adam’s production company...in grand style! The company spent a lavish amount to make the party memorable and opulent. For example, there were tons of interesting attractions, such as an ice skating rink, a bowling alley, and a live performance by the Electric Light Orchestra.

1 Steeply-Priced Golf Cart

Adam Sandler has an intense love for luxury golf carts. He owns a costly one and has been clicked driving his daughters around in it on the weekends. He owns a Pennwick golf cart, which is considered the most exclusive and expensive golf cart customizer in the world.
