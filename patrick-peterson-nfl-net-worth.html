Patrick Peterson is an American football player currently playing for the Arizona Cardinals. As of April 2014, his net worth is estimated to be around $9.5 million.

Patrick Peterson started his career as a running back and defensive back for the Blanche Ely High School's football team. In 2007, he was named by USA Today as High School Defensive Player of the Year.

Upon stepping into Louisiana State University, Peterson played for the LSU Tigers from 2008 to 2010. During his stint with the team, he received Thorpe Award and Bednarik Award in 2010.

In 2011 NFL Draft, Peterson was chosen fifth overall by the Arizona Cardinals. He set the record with the most punt return touchdowns in a single season.

Patrick De'mon Peterson was born on July 11, 1990 in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. He is married to Antonique Larry on June 16, 2012.
