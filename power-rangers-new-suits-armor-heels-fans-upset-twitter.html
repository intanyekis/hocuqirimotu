Fans Are Outraged Over The Armor &amp; Heels Hollywood Life

Instead of shouting “Go! Go! Power Rangers,” fans of the monster fighting teens were shouting “No! No!” when they say the new outfits. Instead of the spandex suits from the television show, the new costumes are “an alien costume that grows on [the Rangers,] that’s not man-made,” production designer Andrew Menzies told Entertainment Weekly. “You can’t win everyone over, but we are trying to appeal to a more mature audience and gain new fans.”

It seems “more mature” means giving the women heels and visible C-cups. What’s even more surprising is that while the male Rangers (played by Dacre Montgomery, 21, RJ Cyler, 21, and Ludi Lin, 28) are wearing some high-tech Adidas, Becky G, 19, and Naomi Scott, 22, will try to battle Elizabeth Banks‘ Rita in wedges!

“The female Power Rangers also wear heels because of course they do,” one upset fan wrote on Twitter, while another remarked how “alien technology” knew to give wedges to the women rangers. Other fans were just ready to ditch the new movie, wondering “who the f*ck signed off on” the decision. Some fans even brought up Star Wars and how Captain Phasma, a female warrior, proved that not all women need a titanium bra.
