Christopher Paz Obituary, Idaho Falls ID, A Valued Member Of The Community Has Died

0SHARESShareTweet

Christopher Paz Obituary, Death –  In a heartbreaking turn of events, Christofer Gonzalo Paz, a vibrant 15-year-old from Idaho Falls, passed away on January 28, 2024, surrounded by his loving family at Eastern Idaho Regional Medical Center. Christofer, born on October 19, 2008, in Idaho Falls, was the beloved son of Gonzalo Paz and Dulce Maria Medrano Paz. Growing up in Idaho Falls, he attended local schools and was a student at Thunder Ridge High School.

Active Community Member

A devout member of Christ the King Catholic Church, Christofer actively participated in various community activities. He was not just a student; he was a passionate member of PFC Soccer and Thunder Ridge Soccer. His enthusiasm for soccer was evident to all who knew him. In addition to sports, he found joy in interests like cars, drawing, fishing, and music. Christofer’s family meant the world to him, and he enjoyed spending quality time with his mother, Dulce Maria Paz, his father, Gonzalo Paz, his sister, Sofia Paz, and his brother, Steven Paz. His uncle, Jorge Medrano, was also an important figure in his life. Surrounded by numerous aunts, uncles, and cousins, Christofer experienced the warmth of a tight-knit family.

Final Farewell and Services

Services to honor Christofer’s memory will be held at Christ The King Catholic Church on Tuesday, February 6, 2024, at 11:00 a.m. Prior to the funeral, a rosary will be conducted on Monday evening at Wood Funeral Home East Side, starting at 6:00 p.m., with visitation until 8:00 p.m. The burial will take place at Fielding Memorial Park Cemetery. As the community mourns the loss of this young soul, they come together to remember the vibrant and cherished life of Christofer Gonzalo Paz, whose impact will forever be etched in the hearts of those who had the privilege of knowing him.
