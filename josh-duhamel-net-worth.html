American actor Josh Duhamel is a former fashion model with an estimated net worth of $15 million.

Born on November 14, 1972 in Minot, North Dakota, USA, Joshua David Duhamel's acting career debuted in 1999 with ABC’s All My Children as Leo du Pres. Subsequently, he appeared on NBC's Las Vegas as Danny McCoy. He is also known to appear as the protagonist Captain/Major/Lieutenant Colonel William Lennox on Transformers and its sequels like Transformers: Dark of the Moon and Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen. He is also known to star on Life as We Know It  and Nicholas Spark’s Safe Haven together with Julianne Hough.

Josh Duhamel has also appeared on shows like The Picture of Dorian Gray (2004), Win a Date with Tad Hamilton! (2004), Turistas (2006), The Romantics (2010), When in Rome (2010), Ramona and Beezus (2010), Life as We Know It (2010), New Year's Eve (2011) and Fire with Fire (2012). In 2013, he appeared as Anson on Movie 43 and Alex on Nicholas Sparks’ Safe Haven.

Duhamel is currently married to singer Fergie, Stacey Ann Ferguson in real life. Marrying in January 2009, the couple renewed their wedding vows in January 2010 and on February 2013, the couple is expecting their first child, Axl Jack.
