WWE Elimination Chamber: Becky Lynch vs. Lita Result

is still The Man.

Becky Lynch defended her RAW Women’s Championship against Lita at WWE Elimination Chamber, and the winner would not only leave with the title, but she would also guarantee a spot on the WrestleMania card. Lita presented quite the challenge for the RAW Women’s Champion, but a flurry of offense by the challenger was not enough to defeat Lynch.

Check out a full recap of the match from our live coverage page below:

Lynch immediately tries the Disarm-her but Lita avoids it. Lita lands a series of strikes. Lynch is livid. Head scissors by Lita. Lita calls for the Twist of Fate but Lynch escapes. Lynch sends Lita into the turnbuckle. Lynch focuses her attack on Lita’s previously injured neck. Lita turns Lynch’s suplex into DDT. Lita fires up. Lynch and Lita trade pin attempts. Lynch tries to use the rope but Lita kicks out. Lita almost locks in a sleeper. Lynch gets to the ropes. Powerbomb by Lita. Lita goes up top. Lynch rolls out of the ring.

Lita hits the Stratussphere. Lita hits the OG Dirty Deeds for a near fall. Lynch isn’t moving. Lita sets up a Moonsault. Lynch crotches her on the top rope. Lynch locks in the Disarm-her. Lita gets to the ropes. Lynch hits the Manhandle Slam. Lita gets her foot on the bottom rope. Lynch misses moonsault of her own. Twist of Fate by Lita. Lita hits her Moonsaullt. Lynch kicks out. Lynch hits a Manhandle Slam out of nowhere for the win.

Winner and STILL RAW Women’s Champion, Becky Lynch!

Lita returned to the ring for her first singles match in ten years. She has competed in multi-competitor matches in recent years (including January’s Royal Rumble appearance), but her last singles match was on July 23, 2012, a win over Heath Slater in an impromptu match at RAW 1000. Prior to that, Lita’s last singles match (and her last title match) was at Survivor Series 2006 where Mickie James beat her for the WWE Women’s Championship.
