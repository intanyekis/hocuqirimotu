Shantel VanSanten met her actor boyfriend while filming'Love Blossoms'

American actress Shantel VanSanten went through a series of not so smooth relationships, but she found her destined one in the end. Her keen fans lit the fire to extra curiosity as they thrived on knowing if the duo would be getting married soon.

VanSanten is popularly known for her appearance in the CW teen drama series, One Tree Hill, where she portrays the character of Quinn James. Besides that, she is also a model featured in the magazines Teen Vogue and Seventeen.

VanSanten Is in a Relationship with Actor Victor Webster

In July 2018, VanSanten revealed her dating status with her boyfriend, Victor Webster, as she posted a picture of the silhouette of the two sharing a kiss on Instagram.

The two first met while filming for the 2017 movie Love Blossoms and immediately hit it off and started dating during the film’s production.

Moreover, the couple is very adventurous—they are frequently traveling and taking unique pictures. And they are not hesitant about showing their love for each other on social media sites.

Webster is also frequently showing off his partner, and the two call each other "adventure buddy," which is seriously adorable because it’s nice to think that they’re relying on each other to go through the tide of life together.

VanSanten and her boyfriend enjoy their parts of thrilling moments and are taking it easy with their relationship. Meanwhile, they might also be planning to get married very soon. Let’s hope it won’t be long before the actress introduces him as her husband.

Her Long List of Celebrity Ex-Boyfriends

The American actress has a pretty long history concerning her romantic involvements. The first revelation of her dating history started with Michael Johnson, which lasted for a mere year from 2008 to 2009, reports WDW.  After that, she dated actor James Lafferty from 2009 to 2010.

Besides that, her one boyfriend who caught the great attention of the public was British actor Jon Fletcher. The duo was a frequent member of posh events and was publicly seeing each other, but that didn’t last long. 

VanSanten’s Bio: She Started Her Career as a Teenager

Born on 25 July 1985, actress and model VanSanten was raised in Spring, Texas. She is of Dutch and Norwegian descent.

She attended the all-girls college prep school, Incarnate Word Academy in Houston at an early age. Afterward, she graduated from Texas Christian University in Fort Worth, Texas.

Moreover, she initially began her career as a model for Page Parkes Management at fifteen. She then appeared on NBC’s reality television series, Sports Illustrated: Swimsuit Model Search, where she was one of the finalists.

She eventually appeared in the movie, You and I alongside Mischa Barton. The film had premiered in January 2011 in Russia and a year later in the US.

In 2009, the One Tree Hill actress appeared in the fourth installment of The Final Destination franchise as Lori Milligan. It was the same year when VanSantel was cast as a regular in the drama series, One Tree Hill from the seventh season of the show.

She continued working as she starred in the psychological horror film, Something Wicked as Christine alongside late actress Brittany Murphy. Since 2016, the diva has co-starred in the drama series, Shooter, based on the novel 'Point of Impact.'

Her Height & Body Measurements

As a model, VanSantel makes extra effort to keep her already slender figure more fit and healthy. Embracing a tall height of 5 feet 8 inches (1.76 meters), she hugs a gorgeous hourglass figure with enviable measurements.

But that didn’t come free as the actress works out daily, keeping sure to keep her measurements of 34-25-35 intact. She was also invited to Women’s Health Magazine to share her workout staples.

VanSanten’s Net worth Is in Millions

According to Celebrity Net Worth, VanSanten has amassed a net worth of $5 million. She makes most of her fortune through her successful acting career.

The actress started to earn more after she was featured in Seventeen and Teen Vogue magazines. And her earning skyrocketed when she got her best-known role as Quinn James in One Tree Hill from 2009 to 2012.

Her net worth also came from appearing in the movies, The Final Destination, Something Wicked, The Open Door, and In My Pocket.
