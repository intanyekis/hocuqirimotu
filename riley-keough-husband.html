Riley Keough Husband, Ben Smith Petersen: Where Is He Now?

Ben Smith-Petersen, Riley Keough’s husband, is a seasoned stuntman and actor from Australia. His performances in internationally acclaimed movies such as The Battle of Five Armies and The Great Gatsby speak volumes about his flair for art.

Smith-Petersen has a great love of music and he even voiced for the song Sand Snapper back in 2013, which earned him considerable recognition. 

Furthermore, Smith-Petersen’s acting ability was evidently spotted by the creators of Triple Frontier who chose him to feature in their crime drama.

Sadly, on 12 January 2023, Riley Keough’s mother Lisa Marie passed away after her death was announced by Priscilla Presley.

Riley opened up about her husband in an interview.

Two years later, Smith-Petersen proposed and announced the good news on Facebook, posting a selfie of his then-fiancée wearing her engagement ring that he had created from a diamond his mother had given him as a gift. 

In February 2015, the actress from Daisy Jones & The Six and Smith-Petersen exchanged vows in charming Napa, California.

‘Ben received his black belt in BJJ today. He achieved his lifelong goal of becoming a black belt before turning 30. I’m very proud of you,’ the War Pony actress captioned her husband’s Jiu-Jitsu victory in April 2021 on Instagram. 

Smith-Petersen routinely posts romantic photos of the married couple on social media.

Who is Ben Smith?

Australian actor and stuntman Ben Smith-Petersen hails from Byron Bay. 

He is well-recognized for being Riley Keough’s husband. He collaborated with Riley on “Mad Max: Fury Road” and took part in several of the risky stunts. 

He has a deep love for music. He contributed vocals for him to a song on the Sandpaper music album (2013). In Dixieland and The Hunger Games: Catching Fire, he performed other playing roles. 

In the animated movie Peter Rabbit, he performed as a stunt double. Petersen also made an appearance in the Triple Frontier criminal drama movie.
