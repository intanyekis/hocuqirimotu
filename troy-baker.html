Biography

Troy Baker was born in Dallas, TX on April 1, 1976. Voice actor who provided the voice of Snow Villiers in Final Fantasy XIII and Booker DeWitt in BioShock Infinite. He also provided the voice of Hawkeye in Avengers Assemble and Joel in The Last of Us. He worked with Tara Platt on several anime shows, including Naruto and Bleach. He began voice acting for radio ads, before moving on to anime.
