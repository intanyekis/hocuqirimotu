Miley Cyrus Bangerz Album Cover Is Totally 80s

Miley Cyrus wears nothing but a sports jacket on the cover of her fourth LP, Bangerz. The 20-year-old pop singer revealed the album artwork with a sexy tweet that read, “You know the vintage YSL don’t stay on for long.” She has come a long way since that Breakout cover. The rest of the Bangerz cover evokes an ’80s vibe. The palms, neon signage and Cyrus’ sport coat immediately recall the late 80s TV series Miami Vice. Cyrus is currently in New York for the MTV Video Music Awards where she will be performing her latest single “We Can’t Stop.” She shared a picture of a teddy bear holding the cover art in her seat for the show at Brooklyn’s Barclays Center via Instagram. Bangerz, with production from Pharrell and Mike Will Made It, will be released on Oct. 8.
