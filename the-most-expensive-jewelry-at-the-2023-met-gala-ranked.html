The 2023 Met Gala was a night to remember, with celebrities from various industries gracing the red carpet in their most exquisite attire. While the gowns and suits were undoubtedly eye-catching, the breathtaking jewelry truly stole the show. As the stars gathered at the Metropolitan Museum of Art, they brought a dazzling array of jewels. Forbes recently published an article highlighting the best jewelry at the event, and this article provides an in-depth look at the best jewelry pieces that left everyone in awe.

The 2023 Met Gala celebrated fashion and art, and the jewelry these celebrities wore only served to elevate their ensembles to new heights. From million-dollar necklaces to luxurious watches, these stunning accessories featured in the 2023 Met Gala will leave a lasting impression.

Elle Fanning - Cartier Collection Grain De Café Necklace - $47,500

Elle Fanning looked radiant in her Cartier Collection Grain de café necklace, priced at $47,500. This unique piece features a series of bean-shaped coffee motifs crafted from 18K yellow gold and 18K white gold set with brilliant-cut diamonds. The necklace's playful design and sparkling diamonds made it a standout accessory at the event.

Kerry Washington - Mikimoto Akoya Cultured Pearl Necklace - $65,000

Kerry Washington made a statement with her Mikimoto Ocean Collection Akoya Cultured Pearl Necklace, valued at $65,000. This stunning piece features Akoya cultured pearls, sapphire, and diamond rondels set in 18K white gold. According to Forbes, the combination of pearls, sapphires, and diamonds created a mesmerizing effect that perfectly complemented Washington's ensemble, including a Butani ring and Joseph Saidian and Sons ring.

Amanda Seyfried - Cartier Agrafe Collection Necklace - $70,000

Amanda Seyfried looked effortlessly chic in her Cartier 18K rose gold Agrafe collection necklace, priced at $70,000. This elegant piece features a series of interlocking loops adorned with brilliant-cut diamonds. The necklace's fluid design and sparkling diamonds made it a stunning addition to Seyfried's look.

Anne Hathaway - Bulgari High Jewelry Monete necklace - $350,000

Anne Hathaway made a bold statement with her Bulgari High Jewelry Monete necklace, valued at a whopping $350,000. This striking piece features an ancient Roman coin set in a diamond-encrusted pendant, suspended from a chain of 18K yellow gold and diamonds. This necklace takes its cue from the sought-after golden Chanel coin adornments that gained fame during the 1980s. The combination of history and luxury made this necklace a true conversation starter.

Anitta - Roger Dubuis’s Velvet Platinum 35 MM Watch - $1.4 Million

Anitta made a bold statement with her choice of wristwear, donning a Roger Dubuis Velvet Platinum 35 MM watch valued at a staggering $1.4 million, as reported by Vogue. This exquisite timepiece features a platinum case, a diamond-set bezel, and a stunning mother-of-pearl dial. This watch was a showstopper on the red carpet, the perfect blend of elegance and sophistication.

Dua Lipa - Tiffany Lucida Star Necklace - $10 Million

Dua Lipa dazzled in her Tiffany Lucida Star Necklace, worth an astounding $10 million as per Robb Report. Dua Lipa was one of the event's co-hosts and was given the privilege of donning one of the most iconic Tiffany creations. The Lucida star necklace is studded with an enormous 100-carat white Legendary diamond that pays homage to the iconic yellow Tiffany diamond. The necklace's simple design allowed the magnificent diamond to take center stage, making it a showstopper on the red carpet.

Rihanna - Bulgari High Jewelry Necklace - $25 Million

Rihanna was the epitome of glamor in her Bulgari High Jewelry Necklace, valued at a jaw-dropping $25 million. This exquisite piece features five pear-shaped diamonds, 109 round diamonds, 118 Akoya cultured pearls, seven pear diamonds, and 561 fancy-shaped diamonds. The necklace's stunning design and exceptional craftsmanship made it a true masterpiece at the event.

Priyanka Chopra Jonas - Bulgari Laguna Blu Diamond Necklace - $25 Million

Priyanka Chopra Jonas looked regal in her Bulgari Laguna Blu Diamond Necklace, valued at $25 million. This incredible piece features the iconic 11.16-carat blue diamond, the largest Bulgari blue diamond available for purchase. The necklace's vibrant blue hue and dazzling diamonds made it a standout accessory on the red carpet. The Laguna Blu was auctioned off for $25 Million at Sotheby's Geneva Luxury Week after a 4-minute bidding war, as per Something About Rocks.

In conclusion, the 2023 Met Gala showcased the finest jewelry in the world. These eight pieces demonstrate the incredible craftsmanship and creativity that goes into creating such stunning accessories. As we look back on this unforgettable event, we can't help but marvel at the dazzling jewels that graced the red carpet, leaving us all in awe of their beauty and splendor. The Met Gala has again proven itself the ultimate stage for showcasing the most luxurious and exquisite jewelry, setting the bar high for future events.
