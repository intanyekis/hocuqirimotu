Professional Acting Career

Adam made his TV debut in 2014 as Kirin Kotecha in the ITV soap opera Emmerdale. He starred in the show until 2016, starring Matthew McNulty and Andrew Scarborough. In 2017, he represented the character of Elijah Stubbs in BBC series In the Dark in two episodes.

Then in 2018, Fielding joined the Channel 4 drama Ackley Bridge, by playing the main role of Aaron Turner. That same year, he also starred in Krypton in one episode. 

Bio and Early Life

Adam Fielding was born on 19 March 1993 in Birmingham, United Kingdom. His mother is of Italian while his father is of Irish heritage. His dad worked in the military. So far, he holds British citizenship and is of mixed ethnicity.
