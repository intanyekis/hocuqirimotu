Diddy's Twins Hang Out With LeBron James Son Bryce In Turks And Caicos

Amid Diddy’s ongoing legal woes, it’s good to see his twin daughters taking a break from it all.

As most teens are enjoying spring break by staying up late, catching up on TV shows, and hanging out with friends, D’Lila Star and Jessie James Combs are apparently vacationing in Turks and Caicos with Bryce James, their school mate and son of LeBron James.

The 16-year-old high school athlete was seen in a since-deleted TikTok video with his friend Boogie Johnson, where they did a dance number by the ocean. Jessie and D’Lila could be seen dancing in the ocean wearing matching bikinis.

See below.

It isn’t much of a surprise that the twins would be hanging out with the two teenage boys, as they all attended homecoming together last year, and seem to be very close.

Boogie even has a photo with one of the twins on his Instagram account, captioned, “Shawty said I’m P I told her I’m V-A-M-P.”

The twins’ official IG page commented two tongue-out emojis.

Unfortunately, having a famous parent sometimes inserts you into their drama by default — even if you’re just a teen trying to live your teenage life.

Diddy’s homes were recently raided by Homeland Security, which also resulted in his sons being placed in cuffs. At the time, the Bad Boy CEO was spotted in Miami. Many gave their take on how the raids were conducted, with some deeming it excessive and unecessary.

Misa Hylton, who shares son Justin Combs with Diddy, spoke out about the unforeseen incident. While sharing a video of the raid on Instagram, she stated, “The over zealous and overtly militarized force used against my sons Justin and Christian is deplorable. If these were the sons of a non-Black celebrity, they would not have been handled with the same aggression. The attempt to humiliate and terrorize these innocent young BLACK MEN is despicable!”

She also noted that she will be taking legal action against Homeland Security.
