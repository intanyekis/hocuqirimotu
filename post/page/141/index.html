published on July 13, 2024

Petrocelli insisted that Scott’s responsibilities concern creative control, performing, and marketing the festival.  “Like any other adrenaline-inducing diversion, music festivals must balance exhilaration with safety and security—but that balance is not the job of performing artists, even those involved in promoting and marketing performances,” Petrocelli expressed in March.
