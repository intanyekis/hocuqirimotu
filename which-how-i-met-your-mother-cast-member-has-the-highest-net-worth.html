Which How I Met Your Mother Cast Member Has The Highest Net Worth

When it comes to television sitcoms, there are a select few shows that have managed to reach massive heights amongst fans, and How I Met Your Mother is one of them! The show first came to prominence back in 2005 and ran for a whopping 7 seasons before it came to an end in 2014.

The show featured an ensemble cast similarly to that of the Big Bang Theory, a show fans say HIMYM has a lot in common with. With Neil Patrick Harris playing the iconic Barney Stinson, Josh Radnor as Ted Mosny, and of course, Colbie Smulders as none other than Robin, fans had their favorites, making the show as successful as it was.

Considering they remained on-air for nearly a decade, there is no surprise that the cast took home the big bucks. While we know how much cast member, Alyson Hannigan is worth, just where does the rest of the cast fall in comparison, better yet, who's the richest?

Who Is The Richest 'HIMYM' Cast Member?

While How I Met Your Mother may no longer be on-air, it's certainly managed to become one of the most successful television shows of all time! After its start back in 2005, fans immediately fell in love with Ted, Barney, Robin, Lily, and Marshall, characters played by some big names including Alyson Hannigan, Neil Patrick Harris, and Colbie Smulders, to name a few.

The sitcom was heavily compared to that of the Big Bang Theory, and Two & A Half Men, and while that would normally create animosity, it was clear that all shows were full of incredible talent, and would all go on to be massive successes throughout the 2000s.

While fans know about the cast members $225,000 per episode paycheck, which comes up to about $5 million per season, it appears as if there's a lot of chatter about who of the cast is the wealthiest! Although they've all done well for themselves, it is none other than Neil Patrick Harris, and Jason Segel that come out on top!

Neil Patrick and Jason Segel tie with a net worth of $50 million each! Harris played the role of Barney Stinson, one of the most iconic characters ever, making it clear as to why he's gone on to amass so much.

As for Jason Segel, the star has not only had success on the show, but he's appeared in countless films, including Forgetting Sarah Marshall, Knocked Up, I Love You, Man, and Bad Teacher, proving just how hot a commodity Segel was during his prime.

Neil Patrick also saw early fame and fortune during his time on Doogie Howser, M.D, and when you throw in his Broadway career, it's no surprise that he and Jason have managed to earn the most coin!
