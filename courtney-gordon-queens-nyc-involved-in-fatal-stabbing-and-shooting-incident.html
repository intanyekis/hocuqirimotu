Courtney Gordon, Queens, NYC, Involved in Fatal Stabbing and Shooting Incident

A devastating incident unfolded in Queens, New York, leaving five dead, including the alleged perpetrator, Courtney Gordon, and two police officers injured. The tragedy has sent shockwaves through the community, prompting a thorough investigation into the events that led to this loss of life.

Courtney Gordon Shooting, What Actually Happened?

In the early hours of Sunday, police responded to a distressing 911 call from a young girl reporting that her cousin was attacking her family. The call led them to a home in Far Rockaway neighborhood, which was ablaze upon their arrival.

The alleged suspect, identified as 39-year-old Courtney Gordon from the Bronx, attacked the arriving officers with a knife. In response, the officers fired shots at Gordon, who later succumbed to his gunshot injuries at the hospital.

A subsequent investigation on the crime scene revealed a horrifying scenario. An 11-year-old girl, a 12-year-old boy, a woman aged 44, and a man in his 30s were found dead, presumably from stab wounds. A 61-year-old woman was also found with multiple stab wounds and immediately taken to the hospital for treatment.

Courtney Gordon: The Suspect

Courtney Gordon, the alleged attacker, had a prior arrest record for domestic violence. His sudden violent outburst resulted in the death of four individuals and his own demise. Following his attack on the responding officers, he was shot and later pronounced dead upon arrival at the hospital.

The Victims and The Aftermath

The victims of this tragic event were believed to be members of the same family. The brutal attack has left the community in shock, and condolences have been pouring in for the grieving family.
