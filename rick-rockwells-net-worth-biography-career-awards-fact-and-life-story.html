Rick Rockwell Net Worth: Rick Rockwell is an American reality television personality who has a net worth of $300 thousand. Rick Rockwell was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania in October 1957. He is best known for being the groom on the 2000 reality television series Who Wants to Marry a Multi-Millionaire?. During the show which was a single airing two hour broadcast, Rockwell chose Darva Conger to be his wife and married her on the spot.

Conger won a 3 carat diamond ring and over $100,000 in prizes. After their honeymoon Conger was granted an annulment claiming that Rockwell had misrepresented himself. Rockwell was billed as a real estate tycoon but later some investigators had claimed that he was not really a multi-millionaire at all. Rick has been involved in show business as an occasional screenwriter and standup comedian. He briefly toured the United States with a standup comedy act riding his fame from the reality show. As an actor Rockwell starred in the films Echoes of the Road, Return of the Killer Tomatoes!, Killer Tomatoes Strike Back!, and Killer Tomatoes Eat France!. He also wrote the first two films in the Killer Tomatoes franchise. He also appeared as a presenter on aviation computer-based training CD-ROM courses from King Schools.
